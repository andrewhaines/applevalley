How It Works

Chasin’ Axe provides turnkey axe throwing solutions for venues and locations wanting to increase foot traffic and revenues by offering a unique entertainment experience.

Axe throwing may sound extreme, but it’s really an easy to learn, safe, fun, and social entertainment option that is becoming increasingly popular among millennial demographics who have high disposable incomes, want authentic thrilling experiences and are increasingly keen to find activities not requiring a computer screen.

With more than 330 axe throwing facilities across the world, this new sport is driving new business to popular venues.

What Is Axe Throwing?

According to legend, the first axe throwing competitions were held competitions were held by the frontiersmen in North America, though there have also been reports of axe throwing competitions amongst the Celtic tribes.

"Urban axe throwing" is a little different. Both a sport and an entertainment activity, it takes place indoors, on a 12’ long range.

Players take turns to throw their axes in order to get as close to the bullseye, or "kill shot" dots at the top of the target.
