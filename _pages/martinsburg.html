FAQs

Q: Do you offer drinks?

A: Yes! We're located inside Pedalin' Pints Craft Beer Pub. They provide full drink service while you're at Chasin' Axe.

Q: Will you teach me how to throw?

A: Of course! Our axe coaches will show you some basic throws to have you sticking shots in no time.

Q: Is there an age limit?

A: Yes, we limit throwers to ages 10 and above. Minors can throw with parental consent / a signed waiver. However, the axes are heavy, and children under 13 might find them hard to handle.

Q: Can I throw my own axe / other things?

A: Personal axes are allowed if they pass our inspection. Other objects may not be thrown.
